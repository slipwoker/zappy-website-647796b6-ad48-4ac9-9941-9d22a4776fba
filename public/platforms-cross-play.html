Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Hello Kitty Island Adventure across all platforms and cross-play features.

Which platforms will Hello Kitty Island Adventure be available on?

Hello Kitty Island Adventure is expanding to all major platforms so no fan gets left behind. You'll be able to play onXbox consoles,Apple Arcadeon iPhone, iPad, and Mac,Android devicesvia Google Play, andSamsung Galaxy phones and tabletsthrough the Galaxy Store and Samsung Apps. Whether you're a console player or a mobile gamer, the island is open for everyone.

What does cross-play actually mean for playing with friends?

Cross-play means your friends don't need to own the same device or console as you to visit your island together. A player on Xbox can hang out, craft, and explore alongside a friend on an Android phone or Samsung Galaxy tablet — all in the same shared world. You'll connect through a single account so your progress, island, and friendships travel with you no matter which screen you pick up.

Are Samsung phones and Galaxy devices fully supported?

Yes! Samsung Galaxy phones and tablets are a core part of our cross-platform rollout. Hello Kitty Island Adventure will be available through theGalaxy StoreandSamsung Apps, optimized to take full advantage of Samsung's display quality and performance. Samsung users will have the same full game experience — including cross-play with Xbox, Apple Arcade, and Android players — right from their Galaxy device.

How does Apple Arcade fit into the cross-play plan?

Apple Arcade players are fully included in the cross-play ecosystem. If you subscribe to Apple Arcade on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac, you'll access the complete Hello Kitty Island Adventure experience and be able to play alongside friends on Xbox, Android, and Samsung devices. Apple Arcade's premium, ad-free environment pairs beautifully with the game's cozy island atmosphere — and your island progress syncs seamlessly across your Apple devices through iCloud.

Will my account and island progress carry over between platforms?

Account continuity is a cornerstone of our cross-platform vision. Your island, friendships, collected items, and progress are tied to your Hello Kitty Island Adventure account — not to a single device. Switch from your Xbox to your Samsung Galaxy or Apple Arcade device and pick up exactly where you left off. We're designing the experience so your island truly belongs toyou, wherever you play.

When will the cross-platform rollout happen?

We're actively working on bringing Hello Kitty Island Adventure to Xbox, Android, and Samsung devices alongside the existing Apple Arcade version. Rollout timelines and platform-specific launch details are being finalized. We'll announce dates and availability as soon as they're confirmed — follow our official channels to stay up to date and be the first to know when your platform goes live.

Still have questions about platform availability, cross-play setup, or the Samsung and Xbox rollout? Our team is happy to help with all the details.
