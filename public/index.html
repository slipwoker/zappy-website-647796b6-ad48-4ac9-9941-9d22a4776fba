One Island, Every Device — Play Together, Anywhere

Hello Kitty Island Adventure is built for the way you actually play. Whether you're on Xbox, Apple Arcade, Android, or Samsung Galaxy, the island is always open — and your friends are always just a tap away. Cross-play makes it effortless to connect, no matter what screen you're holding.

Connect Across Every Platform

Your Xbox squad, your Apple Arcade friends, your Android and Samsung crew — they all share the same island. Cross-play means no one gets left behind, no matter which console or device they call home.

Seamless Sessions Across Screens

Start decorating your island on your Xbox, then pick up exactly where you left off on your phone or tablet. Your progress travels with you, keeping every play session feeling continuous and uninterrupted — even when life pulls you away from the big screen.
