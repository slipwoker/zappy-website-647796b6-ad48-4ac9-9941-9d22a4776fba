About the Adventure

A Warm World Worth Getting Lost In

Hello Kitty Island Adventure invites you to a sun-drenched paradise where friendship is the most powerful currency. Whether you're exploring hidden coves, decorating your island home, or teaming up with Hello Kitty and her Sanrio friends, every moment feels like a gentle exhale — joyful, purposeful, and completely your own.

The island unfolds at your pace. Tend to your garden at dawn, join a friend's island for an afternoon craft session, or spend an evening chasing fireflies along the shore. There are no timers pressing you forward and no pressure to compete — just an ever-expanding world that rewards curiosity and kindness in equal measure.

Made for Every Kind of Player

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or picking up a controller for the first time, Hello Kitty Island Adventure scales beautifully. Families can explore together, while dedicated players uncover deeper layers of island lore and rare collectibles.

One Island, Every Platform

Your island follows you everywhere. Play on Xbox, dive in on Apple Arcade, continue on your Android or Samsung device — cross-play means your friends are always just a wave away, no matter which platform they call home.
